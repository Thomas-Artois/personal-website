Pomodoro project //Automate productivity.

Become more productive by having your work/break schedule automated! 25 minutes of work, followed by 5 minutes of break. Every three work sessions, you get to take a longer break! Just run this program in the background and have it notify you when it's time to go grab a coffee or a breath of fresh air.

Elevator pitch //Present yourself.

Allow this program to present who you are by automatically showing pictures of your choosing, with a counting down timer. No more worries about running out of time when you have to present yourself! Don't go a second over 2 minutes!
