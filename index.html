Pomodoro project //Automate productivity.

Become more productive by having your work/break schedule automated! 25 minutes of work, followed by 5 minutes of break. Every three work sessions, you get to take a longer break!

Built this project with Python as part of the Appbrewery tutorial.

Elevator pitch //Present yourself.

Allow this program to present who you are by automatically showing pictures of your choosing, with a counting down timer. No more forgetting to press a button to go to the next picture in your presentation, let this Python script handle all that business for you!

Built this project with Python as a way to creatively present myself.
